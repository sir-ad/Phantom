# Personas and Jobs-to-be-Done

Owner: PhantomPM PM Team  
Last Updated: 2026-02-13  
Status: Real

## Purpose

Define who PHANTOM serves and what progress they seek.

## Persona 1: Solo Founder

Context:

1. limited PM bandwidth
2. fast iteration pressure
3. technical ownership of product and delivery

Primary JTBD:

1. "Help me make and execute product decisions quickly without losing rigor."

Top pains:

1. prioritization uncertainty
2. unclear tradeoffs
3. repetitive planning and update tasks

Desired outcomes:

1. decision confidence
2. reduced planning overhead
3. clearer execution plan for development

## Persona 2: Startup Product Manager

Context:

1. high communication load across teams
2. roadmap and sprint alignment burden

Primary JTBD:

1. "Help me coordinate product decisions and delivery with less manual overhead."

Top pains:

1. repeated status synthesis
2. incomplete handoffs
3. weak linkage between intent and implementation

Desired outcomes:

1. reusable product artifacts
2. faster alignment cycles
3. consistent decision framing

## Persona 3: Product Engineer

Context:

1. receives vague feature requests
2. responsible for implementation detail quality

Primary JTBD:

1. "Give me product context and acceptance criteria directly in technical terms."

Top pains:

1. ambiguous requirements
2. missing user/value context
3. rework from expectation mismatch

Desired outcomes:

1. clearer execution scope
2. fewer clarification loops
3. better alignment with product outcomes

## Persona 4: Product Lead / Portfolio Owner

Context:

1. multiple tracks/products
2. dependency and risk management complexity

Primary JTBD:

1. "Help me see portfolio health and coordinate strategic delivery decisions."

Top pains:

1. fragmented visibility
2. hidden dependencies
3. inconsistent reporting cadence

Desired outcomes:

1. unified portfolio visibility
2. explicit risk tracking
3. scalable communication workflows

## Prioritization

Initial focus priority:

1. Solo Founder
2. Startup PM
3. Product Engineer
4. Product Lead

Rationale:

1. fastest adoption path
2. strongest pain-to-value immediacy
3. high advocacy potential for early community growth
